# Getting Started

Welcome to TodoPro. This section takes you from an empty account to a working task list in a few minutes.

TodoPro is a task manager that runs on the web, on Android, and in your terminal. All surfaces are backed by one account, so a task you capture on your phone shows up on your laptop right away.

## What to expect

You need three things to be productive:

1. **An account.** Sign up once at [app.todopro.xyz](https://app.todopro.xyz) and every surface uses the same login.
2. **A place to put tasks.** Anything you add without choosing a project lands in your **Inbox**. You can sort it into projects later.
3. **The quick-add syntax.** One line of plain text can set the project, labels, priority, due date, and reminders at once. This is the single biggest time-saver in TodoPro.

## The three steps

**1. [Create your account](create-account.md)**

Sign up with email and password or with Google, then install the apps you want.

**2. [Add your first task](first-task.md)**

Capture something on web, mobile, or the CLI, complete it, and move it into a project.

**3. [Learn quick-add](quick-add-syntax.md)**

Master the natural-language syntax: `#project`, `@label`, `p1`, dates, recurrence, and reminders.

## Pick your surface

You do not have to choose one. Most people use the web app as their main workspace, the mobile app for capture on the go, and the CLI when they are already in a terminal.

See [Apps](../apps/index.md) for what each surface does best, or jump straight to [Web](../apps/web.md), [Mobile](../apps/mobile.md), or [CLI](../apps/cli.md).

!!! tip
    Some features — deadlines, locations, project sharing, the Hub, and the integrations — require TodoPro Pro. There is a 7-day free trial. See [TodoPro Pro](../pro/index.md).
