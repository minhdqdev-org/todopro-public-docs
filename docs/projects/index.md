# Projects

A project is a container for tasks that belong together — a piece of work, an area of your life, or a shared effort with other people.
Everything in TodoPro lives either in a project or in your Inbox.

## Inbox vs projects

The **Inbox** holds every task that has no project.
It is the right place to capture something quickly when you do not want to stop and decide where it belongs.

Projects are where work gets organized.
Move a task out of the Inbox and into a project when you know where it fits, or send it straight to a project when you capture it by typing `#ProjectName` in quick add.

!!! tip
    Capture first, organize later. Type the task into the Inbox now, then sort your Inbox once a day.

## What a project can do

- Hold tasks, grouped into [sections](projects-and-sections.md).
- Show its tasks as a [list or a board](views-and-layouts.md).
- Be [shared with other people](sharing-and-collaboration.md) (Pro).
- Carry free-form notes in scratchpads.
- Be exported to CSV, or filled from a CSV import.
- Publish a read-only [calendar feed](../integrations/calendar.md) you can subscribe to.
- Be saved as a [template](templates.md) and reused.

## How many projects you can have

| Plan | Projects |
| --- | --- |
| Free | 10 |
| Pro | 100 |

Archived projects still count toward your limit until you delete them.
If you hit the limit, archive or delete a project you have finished, or see [TodoPro Pro](../pro/index.md) for the higher allowance.
