# Productivity

Capturing tasks is only half the job. TodoPro also helps you decide what to work on today, stay focused while you do it, and look back at what you actually finished.

## In this section

- **[Today, Upcoming and Review](today-and-upcoming.md)** — the daily planning views: what is due today, what is coming up, what landed in your Inbox, and what needs a second look.
- **[Focus and Pomodoro](focus-and-pomodoro.md)** — run timed focus sessions on a single task, set focus goals, and track your streak of deep work.
- **[Reporting and analytics](reporting-and-analytics.md)** — completion statistics, productivity trends, per-project stats, streaks, and exports.

## A simple daily rhythm

1. Start in **Today**. Clear or reschedule anything overdue, then work down the list.
2. Use **Focus** when a task needs uninterrupted attention.
3. Check **Upcoming** at the end of the day to see what tomorrow looks like.
4. Once a week, open **Review** to catch tasks that have been sitting untouched, and **Reporting** to see how the week went.

!!! tip
    Empty your **Inbox** regularly. Anything captured without a project lands there, and moving those tasks into real projects keeps your lists trustworthy.
