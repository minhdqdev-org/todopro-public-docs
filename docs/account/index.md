# Your account

Everything about your TodoPro account lives in **Settings** in the web app.
One account backs every surface — web, mobile, and the CLI — so a change you make here applies everywhere.

Related pages:

- [Sign-in and security](security.md) — passwords, Google, passkeys, devices, and API tokens.
- [Import and export](import-export.md) — get your data in and out.
- [TodoPro Pro](../pro/index.md) — what Pro includes and how billing works.

## Profile

Open **Settings → Profile** to set:

- **Display name** — the name other people see when you share a project with them.
- **Avatar** — upload an image from your computer or phone.

You can also upload an avatar from the mobile app.

!!! note
    Your avatar is uploaded separately from the rest of your profile, so upload it as its own step rather than expecting it to save with a name change.

## Appearance

**Settings → Appearance** controls how TodoPro looks, including the **light or dark theme**.
The mobile app has its own theme toggle in its settings.

Some display preferences are not part of your profile at all.
Layout (list or board) and sort direction are remembered per device, so you can use a board on your laptop and a list on your phone.
See [Views and layouts](../projects/views-and-layouts.md).

## Notifications

**Settings → Notifications** is where you choose how TodoPro reaches you.
These preferences decide how you receive:

- **Reminders** on your tasks — see [Reminders](../tasks/reminders.md).
- **Project invitations** and other collaboration activity — see [Sharing and collaboration](../projects/sharing-and-collaboration.md).
- **New-device sign-in alerts** — see [Sign-in and security](security.md).

On mobile, notifications arrive as push notifications that deep-link straight to the task.
There is also a **notifications inbox** in the app so you can catch up on anything you missed.

### Daily digest

TodoPro can send you a **daily digest email** summarising what is on your plate.
Turn it on or off in **Settings → Notifications**.

!!! tip
    If reminders are not reaching you, check this page first — the notification channel may simply be switched off. See [Troubleshooting](../troubleshooting.md).

## Billing

**Settings → Billing** shows your current plan and is where you start a **7-day Pro trial**.

- To compare plans, see [TodoPro Pro](../pro/index.md) and the pricing page at <https://todopro.xyz/pricing>.
- Paid subscriptions are managed in a billing portal, where you can update payment details or cancel.
- If you cancel, your Pro access continues to the end of the period you have already paid for.

!!! info "Mobile purchases"
    You can start a trial directly in the Android app, but you finish the purchase on the web.

When your Pro access ends, your data stays put.
Nothing is deleted — Pro-only features simply stop being editable, and you fall back to the Free limits.
See [TodoPro Pro](../pro/index.md) for the full list of what Pro unlocks.

## Deleting your account

If you want to leave, you can delete your account from **Settings**.

Deletion is not instant:

1. You request deletion.
2. Your account is scheduled for removal with a **30-day grace period**.
3. During those 30 days, you can **cancel the deletion** and pick up exactly where you left off.
4. After 30 days, your account and its data are removed.

!!! warning
    Once the 30-day grace period has passed, deletion cannot be undone.
    Export your data first if you may want it later — see [Import and export](import-export.md).

### Cancelling a scheduled deletion

Sign in again within the 30 days and cancel the pending deletion from **Settings**.
Signing in on its own does not cancel it, so make sure you complete the cancellation step.

## Purging your data

There is a separate **purge data** action for when you want to empty your account but keep it.
Purging wipes your data while leaving the account itself in place, so you can start again from scratch.

Because it is irreversible, purging asks you to **re-enter your password** to confirm.

!!! warning
    Purging removes your tasks, projects, and related content.
    Take a full export first if there is anything you want to keep — see [Import and export](import-export.md).
